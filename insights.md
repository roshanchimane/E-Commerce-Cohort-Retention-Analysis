# Key Insights & Business Takeaways

## Customer Retention
- Month 1 retention is ~33–42%, indicating a significant drop after the first purchase.
- Retention consistently declines as cohort index increases, which is expected in e-commerce.

## Customer Value
- Repeat buyers contribute the majority of total revenue.
- Average CLV is significantly higher for retained customers compared to one-time buyers.

## Order Behavior
- Most customers place between 4–7 orders.
- A small group of customers shows high order frequency, indicating potential loyalty segments.

## Churn Observation
- High early churn suggests the need for better onboarding, offers, or follow-up campaigns after the first purchase.

## Business Recommendations
- Introduce post-purchase engagement strategies to improve Month 1 retention.
- Target repeat buyers with loyalty programs.
- Use cohort insights to identify high-value acquisition months.
